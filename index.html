Privacy Policy for Buy Reality

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Buy Reality (“the Extension”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) is a Chrome extension that helps Shopee Malaysia shoppers understand recurring buyer review patterns on Shopee product pages.

This Privacy Policy explains what information Buy Reality processes, how it is used, and how it is protected.

1. Information We Process

Buy Reality may process the following categories of information when you use the extension:

A. Website Content

When you visit a Shopee Malaysia product page, the extension may read publicly visible product page content and product review text necessary to generate buyer-pattern summaries.

This may include:

  • product review text
  • product identifiers such as item ID and shop ID
  • product page context required to generate AI summaries

B. User Activity and Extension Usage Data

Buy Reality may collect limited extension usage analytics to operate, improve, and measure the performance of the extension. This may include:

  • extension events such as extension load, summary visibility, and feedback interactions
  • session ID
  • visitor ID
  • event timestamp
  • non-sensitive product interaction metadata related to extension usage

2. How We Use Information

We use the information described above only for the following purposes:

  • to analyze Shopee review content and generate buyer review summaries
  • to display recurring buyer traps, buyer signals, and confidence signals on Shopee product pages
  • to improve extension functionality, reliability, and user experience
  • to monitor extension performance and usage analytics

3. Third-Party Services

Buy Reality uses third-party infrastructure and service providers to operate the extension, including backend and analytics services.

Publicly visible Shopee review content and limited extension usage data may be transmitted to our backend service providers solely for the purpose of:

  • generating AI-powered review summaries
  • storing analytics events
  • supporting extension functionality

We do not sell user data.

We do not use or transfer user data for purposes unrelated to the extension’s single purpose.

We do not use or transfer user data to determine creditworthiness or for lending purposes.

4. Local Storage

Buy Reality may use browser local storage to store limited local data necessary for extension functionality and performance, including:

  • language preference
  • cached review analysis results
  • locally generated visitor identifiers used for extension analytics

5. Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary to provide, maintain, and improve the extension and its related services.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information processed by Buy Reality. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.
